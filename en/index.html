What we do

For Enterprises

We manage very high trafic websites using reliable PaaS technologies to deploy peacefully and scale automatically applications that we have optimized for performance and robustness (caching, background jobs).

For Startups

We build MVPs by iterating shortly and guiding you through technological choices for quick prototyping, short time-to-market and activity monitoring until you are successful enough to have your own internal product team.

Code quality

Our code is versioned, organised, and tested within continuous integration, continuous audit and continuous deployment processes.

Agility

Our terms (in French) are agile development contracts.
And the code we write belongs to you.

Pricing

As we are working the agile way, invoicing is
simple and transparent: € 75 / hour.
